## Objective

Create independent job files for deputies and voting scrapers with
Bree-compatible exports. Each job runs on its own schedule with try-catch
isolation to prevent cascade failures.

**Purpose**: Implement per-source job isolation where each scraper runs
independently at different times (2 AM and 3 AM UTC) without affecting each
other.

**Output**:

- `apps/ingestion/src/jobs/deputies.ts` with try-catch wrapped scraper logic
- `apps/ingestion/src/jobs/voting.ts` with try-catch wrapped scraper logic
- Updated job registry enabling both jobs with correct cron expressions
- Each job can execute standalone or as part of scheduler
